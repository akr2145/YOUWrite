Howit Works

  1. YouWrite matches students with editors. In order to submit an essay for editing, a student must first edit another student’s essay. As students edit essays, they build their own profiles.

    • If a student consistently gives amazing edits, they can earn a gold nugget.

    • If a student consistently gives good edits, they can earn a silver nugget.

    • If a student consistently gives average edits, they can earn a bronze nugget.

Why us?

YouWrite believes in the power of the masses. The more essays you edit, the more edits you receive. We aim to create a robust community, where your essays will undergo multiple rounds of edits from different students. In addition to giving you more advice, having more editors corrects for any biases resulting from differing qualities of editors. We’ll give you all the resources you need to nail those progressions!

TheHonor Code

All members of the YouWrite community must exhibit academic honesty. In other words, don’t plagiarize / pass off other people’s ideas as your own. Failure to adhere to our standards will result in expulsion from the community and / or academic punishment.
